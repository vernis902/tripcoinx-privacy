Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how TripCoinX ("the app", "we") handles information when you use our Android application. TripCoinX is a simple offline currency calculator for travelers.

Data We Collect

TripCoinX does not collect, store on any server, or transmit any personal data.

The app stores the following settings locally on your device only, using Android SharedPreferences:

  • Your selected source and target currencies
  • The exchange rate value you have entered manually

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

TripCoinX does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting tools.

The app uses Google Play Billing Library to process in-app purchases. When you make a purchase, Google processes the transaction on its own infrastructure. This data is handled by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. No personal information is collected by us, so none can be sold, traded, or transferred.

Data Retention and Deletion

All local app data (currency settings and exchange rate) is stored only on your device. You can delete it at any time by:

  • Going to Settings → Apps → TripCoinX → Clear Data, or
  • Uninstalling the app

Children's Privacy

TripCoinX does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
